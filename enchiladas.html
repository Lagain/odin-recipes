enchiladas!

Description

This recipe basically just involves some chicken and some tortillas, you know? Just a little pasta pizzoli. Well not pasta, because it's enchiladas. I think the ones in the picture look pretty good, personally my boyfriend makes some killer enchis with cream of chicken and green chiles. Divine.

Ingredients

  • Corn tortillas (or flour)
  • Chicken
  • Cheese (any kind, the more the merrier, maybe not gouda? skip gouda
  • Love (you can NOT make enchiladas without love, it is not realistic.)

Steps

  1. Grab that chicken and cook it. You can't eat raw chicken. You can try if you want
  2. You should probably brown the tortillas a bit and then maybe just like put the chicken in them? I'm guessing haha
  3. Cheese on top never stop cheese on top never stop chee
